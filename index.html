    About this dashboard

    What this app does

    This dashboard converts live disruption signals into a policy recommendation for days of cover on the Savannah to Atlanta replenishment lane.

    How to use this

    • Use Live Signal to see the current API-driven readout.
    • Use What-if Test to test hypothetical stress conditions without changing live risk.
    • Compare your selected plan against the 2% target and the recommended days of cover.

    Why this matters

    Small policy changes can sharply reduce stockout risk during disruption. The dashboard is designed to support quick operator decisions without requiring model fluency.

    Assumptions and limits

    This is a model-based estimate, not observed real stockout data. It does not directly ingest real inventory position, truck GPS/ETA feeds, or historical stockout logs.

    Model logic

    The endpoint combines National Weather Service alerts, Georgia 511 traffic events when active, and a baseline port factor into a stress score. The frontend maps that stress regime to policy curves and target checks.
